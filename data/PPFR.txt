The supplementary materials for paper entitled "Prediction of protein folding rates from primary sequences using hy-brid sequence representation" include two datasets used to design and evaluate PPFR method.
Both datasets include the following information:
- sequence index
- PDB id
- primary sequence
- length of the sequence
- folding kinetics types
- experimental folding rate
Note that the explicit prediction model and information that can be used to duplicate PPFR method can be found in the paper.
